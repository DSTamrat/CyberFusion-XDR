👉 Risk Scoring

Risk Scoring Engine Overview

The CyberFusion‑XDR risk engine calculates a dynamic risk score for every entity — users, hosts, applications, and network assets. Scores are derived from alert severity, anomaly frequency, behavioral deviations, and historical baselines.

How Risk Scores Are Computed

  • Alert severity weighting (Critical, High, Medium, Low)
  • Anomaly detection from behavioral analytics
  • MITRE ATT&CK technique mapping
  • Historical risk trends and baselines
  • Contextual enrichment (identity, device, geolocation)

Why Risk Scoring Matters

Risk scoring helps analysts prioritize investigations, reduce noise, and focus on high‑impact threats. It also supports automated response workflows and SOC triage.

Outputs

  • Entity risk score (0–100)
  • Risk category (Low, Moderate, High, Critical)
  • Top contributing factors
  • Recommended actions
