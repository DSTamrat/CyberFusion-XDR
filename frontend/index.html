About CyberFusion‑XDR

CyberFusion‑XDR is an integrated Security Operations Center analytics platform designed to unify detection engineering, risk scoring, threat hunting, behavioral analytics, and DevSecOps automation into one cohesive system.

The platform ingests multi‑source telemetry, enriches events, applies detection logic, calculates dynamic risk scores, and presents insights through dashboards and visual analytics. It is built for modern SOC teams who need speed, clarity, and automation.

Core Capabilities

  • [Detection Engineering](ca://s?q=Explain_detection_engineering) — rule‑based, anomaly‑based, and ML‑driven detections
  • [Risk Scoring](ca://s?q=Explain_risk_scoring) — dynamic scoring using alerts, anomalies, and entity behavior
  • [Threat Hunting](ca://s?q=Explain_threat_hunting) — hypothesis‑driven hunts across logs and events
  • [Dashboards](ca://s?q=Explain_dashboard_architecture) — SOC metrics, trends, and visual analytics
  • [DevSecOps](ca://s?q=Explain_DevSecOps_pipeline) — automated scanning, CI/CD, and secure deployments

System Overview

CyberFusion‑XDR combines detection, risk scoring, dashboards, and DevSecOps pipelines.
