Loan Risk & Financial Analytics

CyberFusion‑XDR includes a financial analytics module originally developed for loan risk scoring and repayment behavior modeling. This module demonstrates how the platform can be adapted for non‑security use cases such as credit risk, fraud detection, and financial forecasting.

Loan Analytics Features

  • Loan status tracking (active, delinquent, closed)
  • Repayment history visualization
  • Borrower risk scoring
  • Fraud anomaly detection
  • Predictive modeling for repayment probability

Outputs

  • Loan risk score
  • Repayment probability
  • Borrower behavior anomalies
  • Portfolio‑level risk distribution

Loan Overview

Shows loan status, repayment history, and risk‑adjusted decisions.
