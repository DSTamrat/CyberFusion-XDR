👉 Analytics

Analytics & Dashboards

The analytics module provides visual dashboards for SOC operations, entity behavior, alert trends, and risk score distributions. These dashboards help analysts quickly understand the security posture and identify emerging threats.

Dashboard Components

  • Alert volume over time
  • Risk score distribution
  • Top MITRE ATT&CK techniques triggered
  • Entity activity heatmaps
  • Threat hunting insights

Why Dashboards Matter

Dashboards transform raw telemetry into actionable insights. They support SOC triage, executive reporting, and continuous monitoring.

Key Metrics

Threat counts, risk score distribution, alert trends, and more.
